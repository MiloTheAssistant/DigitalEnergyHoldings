# Digital Energy Holdings Branding Image Design

## Goal

Create a refreshed Digital Energy Holdings brand image system with a human-centered profile stamp and clean photorealistic platform backgrounds for website and social use.

## Approved Direction

The profile image should feel human-centered without turning a person into the main identifiable subject. It should read as a premium brand stamp: anonymous executive presence, controlled posture, secure asset infrastructure, warm gold light, subtle cyan signal detail, and enough circular-crop safety to work as an avatar on every platform.

The platform backgrounds should remain clean photorealistic scenes. They should depict DEH's existing visual language: disciplined compute infrastructure, governed digital assets, executive command environments, premium black/slate materials, warm metallic gold, and restrained cyan signal lines.

## Quality Rules

- Photorealistic, premium, and architectural.
- No fake readable UI text.
- No logos in the generated scene.
- No watermarks.
- No people as the main subject except anonymous or background human presence for the profile stamp.
- No cluttered stock-photo feel.
- Safe negative space for overlays and platform UI chrome.
- Avoid crypto coins, stock tickers, humanoid robots, hype visuals, and busy dashboards.

## Asset Roles

- Website profile: one primary human-centered brand stamp plus two backup profile options.
- Social profile images: reuse the primary human-centered stamp for platform consistency.
- Facebook, LinkedIn, and X headers: wide clean photoreal backgrounds with left-side negative space.
- Instagram and TikTok vertical images: clean photoreal infrastructure scenes with upper or lower negative space for platform overlays.
- Feed/link images: clean photoreal 16:9 or 1.91:1 backgrounds, no baked-in marketing copy.

## Repo Hygiene
